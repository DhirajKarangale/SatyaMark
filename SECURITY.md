# Security Policy

SatyaMark takes security seriously.  
As an open-source verification infrastructure, responsible disclosure helps keep the ecosystem safe for everyone.

---

---

## Disclosure Process

- Valid vulnerabilities will be fixed and released responsibly
- Credit will be given to the reporter unless anonymity is requested
- Rejected reports will receive a clear explanation

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- Core SatyaMark backend services
- Verification pipelines (text & image)
- Official SDKs and demo applications
- Deployment configurations provided in this repository

Third-party services and forks are out of scope.

---

## Responsible Disclosure

We appreciate coordinated disclosure and ask that you:
- Give us reasonable time to address the issue
- Avoid public disclosure before a fix is released

Thank you for helping keep SatyaMark secure.
